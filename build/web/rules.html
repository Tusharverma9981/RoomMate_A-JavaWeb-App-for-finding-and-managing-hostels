RoomMate - Hostel Owner Guidelines

Rules & Regulations for Listing Hostels

As a property owner on RoomMate, you are required to follow these rules to maintain quality and trust on our platform:

  • Ensure that the property details provided are accurate and up to date.
  • Upload clear and real images of the hostel or home.
  • Maintain cleanliness and hygiene standards in the listed property.
  • Rental pricing should be fair and clearly mentioned with no hidden charges.
  • Provide clear information on facilities like Wi-Fi, meals, security, and electricity.
  • Specify the house rules for tenants (e.g., curfews, guest policies, noise restrictions).
  • Respect the privacy and safety of the students and tenants.
  • Respond promptly to tenant inquiries and maintain good communication.
  • Legal compliance with local rental laws and property guidelines is mandatory.
  • RoomMate reserves the right to suspend listings violating any of these rules.

By listing your property on RoomMate, you agree to abide by these terms and help us build a trustworthy community.
